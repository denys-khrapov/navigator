Oleg Zaidiner

CEO & Co-Founder

Biography

Oleg Zaidiner is an outstanding entrepreneur and creative video marketer specializing in product and brand promotion on the Amazon platform. He is one of the key members of the CEO & Co-Founder team at a leading company specializing in strategic promotion and business optimization on this popular online retail platform.

Oleg was born in Estonia in 1980 and showed an early interest in entrepreneurship. His passion for new technologies and e-commerce led him to establish his own business, and he quickly gained a reputation as an expert in Amazon sales.

He graduated from the Estonian University of Economics and Management with a degree in marketing and business management. After successfully completing his education, Alexander began his career in e-commerce and marketing, working with various companies and brands.

Together with his business partner, Alexander focused his attention on Amazon, recognizing the enormous potential of this online platform for business growth. They founded a company specializing in strategic promotion and optimization of products and brands on Amazon. Alexander Ivanov is considered one of the leading experts in this field, and his strategies have helped numerous clients achieve outstanding results and increase sales on Amazon.

As the CEO & Co-Founder of the company, Oleg Zaidiner leads a team of professionals, develops innovative approaches to product promotion on Amazon, and constantly strives to improve client results. His passion for technology and the continuous search for growth opportunities make him a key player in the e-commerce and Amazon promotion industry.
